What is TNL Media?

TNL Media was started by Tyler Kumar, a passionate media artist with a love for videography, photography and graphic design. The company started with Mr. Kumar solely then developed into a team of like minded individuals who eventually created a media company with years of experience working with many clients across Trinidad and Tobago. TNL Media is here for all of your digital media needs! We specialize in Photography, Videography, Video Editing, and Graphic Design.
